Hi ! My name is Eytane Debruyne, I'm a French based digital artist, specialized in 3D

About my skills

I've been building these skills for 4 years

  • 3D

    3D designer is my current position at Hi Agency. I'm creating on blender, and i'm trying to take as much time as possible to learn Maya, Houdini and industry standard software to reinforce and diversify my 3D skills.

  • Photo Manipulation

    Adobe Photoshop is the first photo manipulation software i've ever used. I use it to process and correct pictures. This kind of photo manipulation softwares are involved in my still 3D image production.

  • Video Editing

    I started using Premiere Pro to edit videos for a youtube channel. I'm now beginning to use Davinci Resolve and After effects for my 3D pipeline. Color grading and color scienence are fascinating to me, I learn more about it everyday

  • Development

    I studied IT for 1 year in highschool, and I since discovered an interest in coding. I learned about python, javascript, and html5/css3. The part I enjoy the most is HTML because the result is visual. But knowing python is great, it helps me reshape my workspaces to my needs

  • Design

    Design is where every of my skills meet. I have to put up solutions that are esthetical and functionnal at the same time. It happens in web design, product design, etc...

  • Photography

    It's the first form of art i've got myself into. I recently explored film photography, and I love it. But for projects I shoot on digital SLRs, I've been shooting weddings, clothing brands, and portraits. My photography skills are completed with my editing and processing abilities.
