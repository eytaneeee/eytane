Leaving Earth - The Future Of Mankind

Leaving Earth Projects

This project idea came to my mind after years of fascination for space exploration and outter space. Going to space is a dream for me, and i thought to myself "What if humans were to live in space". This idea is nourished by many films like interstellar, gravity or alien. I find it very interesting to try to express my vision of a more or less near future in which humans expedite in space.
