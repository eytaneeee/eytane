Xbox Series X - Trailer recreation

Xbox Series X - Trailer recreation

When i saw the Xbox Series X trailer on youtube, i really wanted to challenge myself in recreating it on blender. I used a 3D asset for the controller, and i modelled the console. Each shot has been animated in separated scenes. And the overall animation is inspired by the original video. I rendered it using cycles and evee, the entire sequence is around 400 frames.
